physiotherapy

Our physiotherapy department is staffed by a team of skilled and compassionate professionals who are dedicated to helping patients recover from physical injuries or impairments. We offer a range of therapies and exercises designed to improve mobility, reduce pain, and enhance physical function. Our personalized approach to care ensures that each patient receives treatment tailored to their individual needs, goals, and abilities.

A physiotherapy department is a healthcare facility that specializes in helping patients recover from physical injuries, impairments, or disabilities through evidence-based therapies and exercises. The department is typically staffed by a team of licensed physiotherapists who are trained in assessing and treating musculoskeletal, neurological, and cardiopulmonary conditions.


The physiotherapy department may offer a range of services, such as rehabilitation for injuries, management of chronic conditions, recovery from strokes, paediatric physiotherapy, and women's health physiotherapy. The treatment plans developed by the physiotherapists are typically personalized to meet each patient's unique needs and goals, and may include manual therapy, exercise therapy, and patient education.

In addition to providing direct patient care, the physiotherapy department may also collaborate with other healthcare providers, such as physicians, occupational therapists, and speech therapists, to ensure comprehensive, coordinated care for patients.
