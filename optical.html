Paeditaric Ophthalmology

Paediatric ophthalmologists are specially skilled in treating kids in a way that makes them feel relaxed and compliant. They employ customised equipment that is sized for children as well as eye tests that are tailored to the child's developmental stage.


Paediatric ophthalmologists provide ongoing care for kids with eye problems like juvenile arthritis or Type 1 diabetes or:


  • Strabismus (crossed or wandering eyes)
  • Amblyopia (lazy eye)
  • Blocked tear ducts

Opthal

An ophthalmology department in a hospital is a specialized unit that provides medical and surgical care for conditions affecting the eyes and vision. This department is typically staffed by ophthalmologists, who are medical doctors with specialized training in the diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders.

Some of the key functions of an ophthalmology department in a hospital include:
1 . Diagnosis and treatment of eye disorders: Ophthalmologists in the hospital diagnose and treat a wide range of eye conditions, including refractive errors, cataracts, glaucoma, macular degeneration, and diabetic retinopathy, among others....
2 . Surgical interventions: Ophthalmologists in the hospital perform a variety of surgical procedures, such as cataract surgery, corneal transplantation, and retinal detachment repair, among others.
3 . Routine eye exams: Ophthalmologists in the hospital also perform routine eye exams to screen for and detect eye conditions, including those that may not have obvious symptoms.
4 . Collaboration with other healthcare providers: Ophthalmologists in the hospital work closely with other healthcare providers, such as primary care physicians and endocrinologists, to manage the care of patients with complex medical conditions that may affect the eyes.
5 . Patient education: Ophthalmologists in the hospital provide education to patients and their families about their eye conditions and treatment options, as well as strategies for maintaining good eye health.
Overall, the ophthalmology department in a hospital plays a crucial role in the diagnosis and treatment of eye conditions, helping to preserve and improve the vision of patients.
